Past Events

RACHANA-22
We have successfully done the huge project competition i.e RACHANA-2022 under ENTESA.In that competition industrial expertise was there to guide a students about their projects and their applications in industries. Other colleges also participated in RACHANA-2022 competition, Software as well as hardware project are done by students in very innovative manners. With the help of industrial expertise along with senior faculties with team analysis of every single project has been done .Most innovative and ideal projects are choosen according to analysis and prize distribution has been done.
Industrial Visit-22
Till now under ENTESA 2 Industrial Visits are went.The first one is to the Pawana Dam and the second one is to the SIGMA. At the SIGMA ,there were mechatronics machine such as pneumatic actuator , hydraulic actuator and different types of sensors were present. At Pawana Dam students get the knowledge of how to generate energy that is electricity from water and relative components. After visiting this places our students gain to more practical knowledge about hardware and machineries.
Induction Program
S.Y. Induction Programme is held from 17 / 8 / 2022 to 19 / 8 / 2022. The very 1st session about patent and IPR was conducted by Dr. S. C. Patil. The session was very informative. The next session was conducted by Mrs. V. S. Kulkarni mam. She introduced us to Tinkercad. Our placement officer Respected Dr. S. P. Raoborde sir interacted with students. He gave 5 mantras for our future journey. In morning of next day we do some yoga and meditation. We felt very energised after this session. Then Rane mam introduced us to matlab application as we are new to that. Dr. P. M. Ghate sir also showed the correct path to our Engineering life by giving us importantance of projects. The last day is all about industrial trip. We visited SSIGMA pvt. ltd. Along with Rathod sir. The overall induction programme was arranged very well by Faculties as well as student coordinators.
