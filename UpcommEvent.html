Blood Donation Camp
On the Occasion of 26/11 that means the "Shahid Day" ENTESA have arranged a "MAHA BLOOD DONATION CAMP". This event will done in cordination with "AKSHAY BLOOD BANK" on 24 November 2023 at Jayawant Library.Every donar will get the refreshment before donating blood. In the blood donation camp single requirement of donar will be fulfilled. After donation of blood donar will get 16 GB pendrive.
Festin-O-2K22
On 26 November 2022 a "Cultural Event" is organised by ENTESA at C-Building-Auditorium. Our main aim is to conduct such a grand event is to develop students stage daring , confidence , to participate in extra curricular activities and so on. We are having ● Singing ● Dancing ● Drama ● Mimicry ● Poetry activities in it. And also we will give the first prize award to the students and certificates to the other participants.
Industrial Visit
On the occasion of " National Science Day " on 28 February 2023 we have arranged a small educational trip that means , Industrial Visit to GMRT ( Giant Metrewave Radio Telescope ) at Khodad ( Narayangaon). Which is in between 21-28 February. GMRT is famous for world's biggest Telescope. So the telecommunication students can get the knowledge of such a things .
Techfest | IIT Bombay
Techfest is the annual science and technology festival of Indian Institute of Technology Bombay, consisting of social initiatives and outreach programs throughout the year. This program is held in between 16 - 18 December 2022. So on the 26 th of December our third year student will report to the IIT Bombay. It's a one day visit so students can gain the maximum knowledge from that event.
AI-ML Workshop
AI refers to the capability of an entity or a machine to exhibit behaviours that resemble human intelligence. Machine learning allows AI to learn from data, offering unprecedented opportunities for analysis, mapping and prediction in any field. After attending this workshop students are able to learn automation. They are able to solve complex problems.
E-Waste Project Competition
E-Waste is the project based competition. It is an electronic products that are unwanted, not working, and nearing or at the end of their “useful life.” Computers, televisions, VCRs, stereos, copiers, and fax machines are everyday electronic products.Creative and environmentally conscious thinkers will have the opportunity to showcase their ideas for recycling e-waste during this event.
